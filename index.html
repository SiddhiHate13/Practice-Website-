Women In Tech

"EmpowerHer", presented by edba academy, is a transformative program dedicated to women and women in tech. Offering free courses in the MERN stack – MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js – EmpowerHer is designed to empower women with the skills needed to thrive in the dynamic world of technology. Join us on this journey of learning, collaboration, and empowerment as we strive to break barriers and foster inclusivity in the tech industry. At EmpowerHer, we believe in shaping a future where women lead, innovate, and excel.
